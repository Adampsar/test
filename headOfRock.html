MEDIA


Head of The Rock

October 8th, 2017

Rockford IL  – The 2017 Fall racing season was kicked off with a trip to the Head of the Rock, just a short drive away in Illinois. Facing Purdue, Illinois, and other teams from across the Midwest, the team used this race as an opportunity to re-acquaint themselves with competition and introduce members of the novice team to collegiate competition.

Row-out conditions Saturday were less than ideal, with boats struggling against rain, chop, and Rockford’s famous driving headwind. Fortunately, conditions for racing on Sunday were much improved, with a calm sunny morning transitioning to mild chop and winds for the final races.

The team put this opportunity to good use, and showed up ready to perform. The first Varsity 8+ took gold by 21 seconds over second place Purdue. Similarly, the Quad and first Varsity 4+ won gold in their respective events. The freshmen showed their mettle by seizing gold in the Novice 8+, as well as gold and bronze in the Novice 4+ event.

The team now prepares for a split weekend on Oct 21-22. Two varsity fours and an eight will be traveling to Boston for the Head of the Charles, while the rest of the squad prepares for our annual head-to- head against Michigan State in East Lansing.

Make sure to keep up to date with the rest of the season by following us on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, or by continued patronage to this website!
