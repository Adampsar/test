COACHING MENTORSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

Each camp will provide a small number of mentorships for coaches who wish to learn more about that camp’s particular focus. The mentorship will allow you to interact intensively with a collegiate coaching staff that has spent years building on of the country’s most successful programs.

Mentorship program coaches will ride along with a Michigan coach during each practice session, engaging in conversation about the coaching approaches being used, both technical and structural. Off water, the mentorship includes significant opportunities to consult with the Michigan staff about the details of your own program.

  • Training schedules
  • Technical & teaching approaches
  • Building cohesive teams
  • Structuring your program for success
  • Regatta travel and trailer management

Camp Dates and Coaching Foci:

Junior Girls High Performance Camp – June 16-21

  • Experienced high school/ junior or new ACRA college coaches

Junior Girls Fundamentals Camp – June 22-25

  • Freshman/ Novice coaches at the high school or collegiate club level

Junior Boys Fundamental Camp – June 25-28

  • Freshman/ Novice coaches at the high school or collegiate club level

Junior Boys High Performance Camp – July 14-19

  • Experienced high school/ junior or new ACRA college coaches

Cost:

The cost of this program for the week-long Junior High Performance Camps is $1300. For the Fundamentals Camps the cost is $850. This includes housing and food, participation in all camp events, and individual consultations on a variety of subjects with the coaching staff.

Registration: To register for any of the coaching mentorship programs please fill out the Application Form in its entirety. Please make sure to select which group you would like to work with for your mentorship.

Coaches Mentorship Application: You will receive an email confirmation indicating that we have received your application. Applications will be reviewed on a first come first serve basis and in order to reserve your spot you must submit a deposit of $250 within 2 weeks of being offered a mentorship.
