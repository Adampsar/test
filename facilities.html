ARGO BOATHOUSE

The Michigan Rowing Boathouse is located in Bandemer Park, about 1.5 miles north of the University of Michigan central campus. We have 3,000 meters of rowable water is located in Argo Pond, a dammed section of the Huron River. The boathouse is about a 10 minute run or bike from central campus.
